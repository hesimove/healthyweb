Diet Plan for Weight Loss and Cardio Exercise

1. Stay hydrated throughout the day; 2. Practice portion control; 3. Maintain consistent meal timing; 4. Balance macronutrients in each meal; 5. Limit intake of sugars and high-fat foods.

Daily Meal Plan for Muscle Building

1. Consume 1.6-2.2g of protein per kg of body weight daily; 2. Eat every 3-4 hours for steady nutrient and energy supply; 3. Drink water all day to support muscle function and recovery; 4. Balance proteins, carbs, and fats in each meal; 5. Consider creatine and other supplements, with professional advice.
